FlappinGo

Flap, dodge, and keep going! Help our quirky flapper avoid sharp spikes, collect all the bubbles, and beat your best score.

Game Highlights

  • Two-button control: tap left or right to navigate
  • Collect all bubbles to complete a level
  • Minimalist style with smooth, fun visuals
  • Fast-paced and perfect for quick bursts of fun
